Using clefs, time signatures, and key signatures

To add or change a clef:

  1. Move your mouse over an existing clef. It will turn red.
  2. If the desired measure doesn't have a clef, move your mouse over the left side of the desired measure. A small caret with a picture of a clef will appear.
  3. Click the mouse button to add the clef shown or to toggle the existing clef.
  4. If you toggle the clef on a measure after the first measure so that it matches the previous clef, it will disappear.

To add or change a time signature:

  1. Move your mouse over an existing time signature, which will turn red.
  2. If the desired measure doesn't have a time signature, move your mouse over the left side of the measure. A small caret with a picture of a time signature will appear.
  3. Click the mouse button to bring up the time signature editing panel.
  4. Change the time signature by typing in the top box or using the up/down arrows, and by selecting a number from the bottom box.
  5. Click the "X" button to close the time signature editing panel.

To delete a time signature:

  1. Move your mouse over an existing time signature, which will turn red.
  2. Press the DELETE key to delete the time signature.

The time signature in the first measure of a song can not be deleted.

To add a compound time signature:

  1. Open the time signature editing panel as described above.
  2. Click the "+" button to change the time signature to a compound time signature.
  3. Edit the second part of the time signature in the same way as the first.
  4. To make a compound time signature back into a simple time signature, click the "-" button.

To add or change a key signature:

  1. Move your mouse over an existing key signature, which will turn red.
  2. If the desired measure doesn't have a key signature, move your mouse over the left side of the measure. A small caret with a picture of a sharp and flat symbol will appear.
  3. Click the mouse button to bring up the key signature editing panel.
  4. Change the key signature by selecting a pitch from the first box, and major/minor from the second box.
  5. Click the "X" button to close the key signature editing panel.

To delete a key signature:

  1. Move your mouse over an existing key signature, which will turn red.
  2. Press the DELETE key to delete the key signature.

Deleting the key signature in the first measure will change the key to C major.
