Using repeats

To add a repeat:

  1. Move your mouse over the far left of the desired measure. An opening repeat symbol appears in red.
  2. Click the mouse button to insert the repeat.
  3. Move your mouse over the measure in which the repeat will end. A closing repeat symbol appears in red.
  4. Click the mouse button to close the repeat.

You must place the closing repeat symbol before performing any more operations on a staff.

To add a repeat:

  1. Move your mouse over either the opening or closing repeat symbol, which will turn red.
  2. Press the DELETE key to delete the repeat.

To change the number of times a section repeats:

  1. Move your mouse over the closing repeat symbol, which will turn red.
  2. Click the mouse button to bring up the repeat editing control panel.
  3. Type the desired number of repeats (at least 2) in the box, or use the up/down arrows.
  4. Click the "X" button to close the repeat editing control panel.
