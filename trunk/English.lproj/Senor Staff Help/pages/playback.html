Playing a song

To play a song:

  1. Click the "Start" button in the toolbar.
  2. The song will begin playing from the first measure.
  3. Click the "Stop" button to stop playback prematurely.

To play part of a song:

  1. Select the notes you wish to play. (See Adding and deleting notes for more information on selecting notes.)
  2. Click the "Play Selected" button in the toolbar.
