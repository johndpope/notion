Adding, deleting, and modifying staffs

To add a new staff:

  1. Click on the "add staff" button in the lower left corner of the window.

To delete a staff:

  1. Click on the "X" button to the left of the staff.

To rename a staff:

  1. Type the new name in the name textbox to the left of the staff.

To transpose a staff:

  1. Type the number of half-steps by which the staff should be transposed (e.g. "+3" or "-12") in the transposition textbox to the left of the staff.
  2. (Alternatively, use the up/down arrow buttons to modify the amount in the box.)
