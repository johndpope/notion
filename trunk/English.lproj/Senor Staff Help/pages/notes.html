Adding and deleting notes

To add a note:

  1. Select the desired note duration from the duration menu in the toolbar.
  2. To select a dotted duration, check the dotted note checkbox.
  3. To select a triplet duration, check the triplet note checkbox.
  4. To tie the note you're about to place to the note before it, select the tied note checkbox.
  5. To add a note with an accidental, check the checkbox next to the desired accidental.
  6. Move your mouse to the desired note position. A blue note will appear under the mouse.
  7. Click the mouse button to place the note.
  8. (Alternatively, move the mouse close to where you wish to place a note, and press the letter corresponding to the note you wish to place.)

To delete a note:

  1. Move your mouse to the desired note, which will turn red.
  2. Press the DELETE key to delete the note.

To move a note:

  1. Move your mouse to the desired note, which will turn red.
  2. Click the mouse button and drag the note up or down to change its pitch.

To move or delete multiple notes:

  1. Click on the first note, which will turn red.
  2. Hold the SHIFT key and click on the last note. All notes in between will turn red.
  3. (Alternatively, choose "select mode" from the duration menu and double-click anywhere in a mesure to select all notes in that measure.)
  4. Drag any of the selected notes up or down to change the pitch of all selected notes.
  5. Press the DELETE key to delete all selected notes.
