Using drum staffs

To create a drum staff:

  1. Select "Drums" from the staff's channel selection menu to the left of the editing window.
  2. A drum staff does not use clefs or key signatures; instead, the drum corresponding to each position on the staff is displayed over the right side of the staff.
  3. Notes, rests, etc. are all manipulated in the same way as on a normal staff.
