Editing drum kits

To edit the drum kit for a drum staff:

  1. Select "Drums" from the staff's channel selection menu to the left of the editing window.
  2. Click the "Edit" button to the left of the staff.
  3. The drum kit editing sheet will appear. The drums available for this staff will appear in order in the right-hand box. All available drums in the MIDI spec are listed in the left-hand box.
  4. Drag a drum from the left-hand box to the right-hand box to add it to the staff.
  5. Drag a drum within the right-hand box to reorder the drums in the staff.
  6. Click on a drum in the right-hand box and press the DELETE key to remove a drum from the staff. Removing a drum for which you've already placed notes may have unpredictable results.

To export the drum kit for a drum staff to a file:

  1. Click the "Edit" button to the left of a drum staff.
  2. The drum kit editing sheet will appear. The drums available for this staff will appear in order in the right-hand box.
  3. Click the "Export" button and select a name and location for the file.

To import the drum kit for a drum staff from a file:

  1. Click the "Edit" button to the left of a drum staff. The drum kit editing sheet will appear.
  2. Click the "Import" button and locate the file.
  3. The drum kit will be loaded from the file and appear in the right-hand box. Changing the drum kit for a staff with notes already placed may have unpredictable results.
