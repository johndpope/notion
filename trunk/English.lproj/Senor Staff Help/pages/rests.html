Adding and deleting rests and chords

To add a rest:

  1. Select the desired duration from the duration menu in the toolbar.
  2. To select a dotted duration, check the dotted note checkbox.
  3. To select a triplet duration, check the triplet note checkbox.
  4. Move the mouse to the desired position. A blue note of the appropriate duration will appear.
  5. Press the SPACEBAR key to place a rest instead of a note.

To delete a rest:

  1. Move your mouse to the desired rest, which will turn red.
  2. Press the DELETE key to delete the rest.

To add a chord:

  1. Place the first note in the chord, as described here.
  2. Move your mouse directly above or below the first note. A blue note will appear.
  3. Click the mouse. The original note will become a chord along with the note you've just placed.

To delete a chord:

  1. Move your mouse over any note in the chord. The chord will turn red.
  2. Press the DELETE key to delete the entire chord.

To delete a single note in a chord:

  1. Move your mouse over the note you wish to delete. The chord will turn red.
  2. Press and hold the ALT/OPTION key. Only the note you are over will be red.
  3. While keeping ALT/OPTION held down, press the DELETE key to delete the note.
