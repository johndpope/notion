Setting the tempo

To set the tempo:

  1. If the measure at which you want to set the tempo doesn't have a tempo designation yet, move your mouse over the bar on top of the editing window to make a tempo designation box appear.
  2. Click in the tempo designation box and type the desired tempo.
  3. Press ENTER or click outside the box for the change to take effect.

To remove a tempo designation:

  1. Click in the tempo designation box and delete the number inside.
  2. Click outside the box for the change to take effect. The tempo designation box will disappear once you move your mouse away.
